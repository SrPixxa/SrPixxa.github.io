About me

Cynthia Umaña is a depression and relationships coach, author, and currently a psychology and counselling student. A single mother of two who, after suffering depression, anxiety, and an eating disorder due to a breakup, shares how she found the way of how all of us have the power to feel "happiness" again, even after the most painful experiences in our lives.

Online Coaching

After leaving with depression, anxiety and an eating disorder that was consuming my life, I discover through mentors and coaching how we can change our lives by using some techniques to work on our mind and body. When we work on our physical health, our mental health just naturally improves. While changing the way we see and think about life, we change our current situations too.

From not being able to stand from my bed, I'm now living my best version of myself and reaching dreams and goals, that I once thought, they were impossible.

Let's work together to reach your goals and dreams too. Anything is possible.
