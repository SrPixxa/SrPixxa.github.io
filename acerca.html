About me

About me

My name is Cynthia Umaña, I’m Colombian and I once moved to the UK for  the love of my life. But one day. His love was gone and my darkest moments of my life started.


After living with deep depression for 11 years, where I was so lost, so empty, unhappy, and at the same time, I created an eating disorder, where food was my escape, I was replacing that happiness that has to be inside us and created by us with sadness and an addiction to over eat to fill my empty space in my heart, a coping mechanism to forget about my pain. I was choosing to think and  live in the past.

In my past there was so much psychological abuse , rejection, insults and I was told how useless and ugly I was that I ended up believing it. And I forgot and abandoned the most important person who we need to love and cheer up and look after which is “ourselves”.  I didn’t have any self love and self esteem.


It wasn’t until I experienced my most painful moment in my life. That I started to realise that these painful moments are there to teach us a lesson. We can grow. Be stronger. And be wise for the rest of my life.

I learnt that self love , forgive myself and others and leave the past where it belongs, and choose very wisely my thoughts was the recipe for happiness. And I am living the happiest I’ve ever been.


I am the owner of my thoughts, and if I choose to be happy, I will, if I choose to only focus in the present and work for my dreams I can reach and make anything possible.
