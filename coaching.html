Online Coaching

1️⃣ After having a deep depression for 11 years, and an eating disorder, feeling so sick all the time in a bed, never looking after my body, choosing the most toxic foods, day and night, to pretend a smile on my face to keep my life with my children going in the middle of so much pain and sadness, to not be able to look for a job, because I was so scared, shy, I felt useless and lost, I didn’t have trust in myself and no confidence so I couldn’t keep any job anyway.

2️⃣ I didn’t even know what I wanted, I was so empty, I didn’t love anything, I couldn’t get out of my bed or house. I couldn’t look at people in the eyes. Because I felt so little, so unimportant. All the rejections, insults and my traumatic experiences in the past were in my thoughts all the time, and I was choosing to live in this past. And it was consuming and stopping me from living in the present. Which is where we need to put our focus on.

3️⃣ And after all these, I am now the happiest I’ve ever been, writing books, being independent, having my own business, loving life and myself.


I went back to study, to gain knowledge and work on myself. I know that if I’m happy, my loved ones will be too.

4️⃣ And I’m telling you all this, because it doesn’t matter what happened to yourself in the past, there is hope!, we can be happy!.


I can help you to defeat all this darkness and sadness. To overcome past stories and to move on and learn to cope in healthy ways the stressful and difficult moments in life.

The past is a place to learn, the present,

the place to enjoy and the future

to be excited about!!

Cynthia Umana
